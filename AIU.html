Albukhary International University

Albukhary International University is a private, non-profit university located in Alor Setar, Malaysia. The university's story began in 1952, amidst a nation striving for independence and progress. Recognizing the critical role of education in shaping the future, a group of visionary individuals came together to establish an institution that would provide quality education and foster intellectual growth. With limited resources but unwavering determination, they laid the groundwork for a university that would cater to the needs of a rapidly evolving society. The initial campus was modest, housing a small number of students and faculty, but it became a symbol of hope and aspiration for a nation eager to embrace knowledge and innovation. As the nation progressed, so too did the university. The institution gradually expanded its offerings, introducing new programs and disciplines to meet the changing demands of the job market. The faculty grew in number and expertise, attracting renowned scholars from around the world. The university's reputation for academic excellence began to spread, drawing students from both domestic and international backgrounds. To accommodate the growing student body, the campus expanded, incorporating state-of-the-art facilities that enhanced the overall learning experience. Beyond its academic pursuits, the university played a pivotal role in shaping the nation's social and cultural landscape. It became a hub for intellectual discourse, fostering critical thinking and promoting a spirit of inquiry. The university's graduates went on to make significant contributions across various fields, from business and technology to the arts and sciences. Additionally, the university actively engaged with the community, participating in social development initiatives and providing outreach programs to underserved populations. Its commitment to service and social responsibility earned it widespread respect and admiration. As the university continued to evolve, it increasingly established itself as a global player in higher education. It forged partnerships with prestigious institutions around the world, facilitating student exchange programs and collaborative research projects. The university's graduates became sought after by multinational corporations and leading organizations, further enhancing its reputation. The university's legacy is one. Albukhary International University (AiU) is fully funded by the Albukhary Foundation and all students attending AiU shall be sponsored through the Scholarship described herein. The Foundation intends to make available ten (10) scholarships for each country to assist disadvantaged youths, mainly Muslim youths, to graduate with a degree from the Albukhary International University. Instituted in 2003, the Foundation has so far offered the Scholarship to more than 400 students from some 50 countries many of whom are currently studying at the International Islamic University Malaysia (IIUM). This Scholarship is now available for students who can apply to begin their studies at AiU in July 2011, starting with the Foundation Studies Programme.
